Navaan Sandhu


Navaan Sandhu is a Punjabi singer born in 1993 who trained at the Gurmeet Bawa Sangeet Academy in Amritsar. He began his career as a composer before gaining global recognition for his hit singles like "In Demand" and "Special Edition" with producer Manni Sandhu. His music is known for fusing contemporary pop with traditional Punjabi hooks, and he has released several successful tracks, including collaborations and EPs.

Early life and training: Born in Amritsar in 1993, Sandhu initially trained as a singer at the Gurmeet Bawa Sangeet Academy.

Music career: He started his career as a composer. He broke onto the global Punjabi scene with the 2018 single "In Demand," a collaboration with UK producer Manni Sandhu. He has released several other hits, including "Special Edition" and "Do Pal".

His music often blends contemporary pop sounds with traditional Punjabi styles, with songs like "Gangster Yaar," "Russia Ton Wadda," and "Nasheyan Ch Patteya Peya" being popular examples.

Other works: He has released multiple singles and extended plays (EPs) and has collaborated with other prominent artists.

Performance style: Sandhu is known for delivering "potent verses over instantly recognisable Punjabi hooks".
